Privacy Policy

Effective 2026-05-21. Last updated 2026-05-21.

Short version: this site uses privacy-friendly analytics that don't set cookies, don't collect personal information, and don't follow you across the web. The longer version below covers every category of data the site touches, what we do with it, and how to opt out.

1. Who we are

PURSUE UFO Tracker (the "Site") is operated by Anthony Fong, a solo independent operator. The Site mirrors and indexes U.S. Department of War PURSUE program disclosures published at war.gov/UFO. The Site is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or operating under the authority of the U.S. Department of War, the Department of Defense, AARO, or any other government agency.

For any privacy question, see the contact page or email anthony.fong.esq@gmail.com.

2. Analytics (Plausible)

The Site uses Plausible Analytics for traffic measurement. Plausible was chosen specifically because it does not use cookies and does not collect personally identifiable information.

What Plausible records

  • The page URL you visited on this site
  • Your approximate country (derived from anonymized IP, not stored)
  • Your browser and operating system family (e.g. "Chrome on Android")
  • The referring page that sent you here (if any)
  • The screen size category (mobile / tablet / desktop)
  • A small number of custom interaction events (e.g. clicking a Share button, clicking a top-card on the homepage)

What Plausible does NOT record

  • Your IP address (used briefly for country lookup, never stored)
  • Cookies of any kind
  • Local storage on your device
  • Cross-site tracking identifiers
  • Personally identifiable information

Plausible's own privacy policy is at plausible.io/privacy. Plausible is operated by Plausible Insights OU, hosted in the European Union, and is GDPR-compliant by default.

You can block Plausible at the network level using any standard ad-blocker or content-blocker. The Site continues to function normally if you do.

3. Advertising (Google AdSense)

As of 2026-05-21, the Site does not serve advertisements. The HTML contains ad slot placeholders for future activation, but the Google AdSense script is conditionally loaded only when a publisher ID is configured, and no publisher ID is configured at this time. No third-party ad scripts are loaded on the Site today.

When the Site begins serving Google AdSense advertisements (anticipated within the coming weeks), this section will be updated to disclose:

  • That Google and its partners use cookies to serve ads based on a visitor's prior visits to this site or other sites on the internet
  • That Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on visitor activity
  • That visitors may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings
  • That visitors may alternatively opt out of a third party vendor's use of cookies for personalized advertising via www.aboutads.info

This Privacy Policy will be updated with the activation date when AdSense goes live. For now, no advertising data is collected.

4. Server logs (Cloudflare)

The Site is hosted on Cloudflare Pages. Cloudflare maintains its own access logs for security and abuse prevention. These logs may include IP addresses, request paths, and user agent strings. The operator of this Site does not access raw Cloudflare logs except to investigate a specific security incident, and does not export, sell, or share them. Cloudflare's privacy policy is at cloudflare.com/privacypolicy.

5. Email / newsletter

The Site does not currently operate an email newsletter. If and when one is launched, this section will be updated to disclose the email service provider, what data is collected at signup, how unsubscribes are handled, and how the list is stored. Until then, the "subscribe" prompts on the Site link only to RSS or to a public GitHub watch page; no email address is collected.

6. Embedded content

Detail pages on the Site may embed media files (PDFs, MP4 videos, thumbnail images) hosted on third-party domains:

  • war.gov (and its CDN at portals/1/Interactive/...): U.S. Department of War, source of record for PURSUE files
  • dvidshub.net / cloudfront.net: DVIDS, the Defense Visual Information Distribution Service that hosts the video MP4s referenced in PURSUE
  • fonts.googleapis.com / fonts.gstatic.com: Google Fonts (the Site uses the Space Grotesk and JetBrains Mono typefaces)

Embedded content from these domains may set their own cookies or collect analytics under their own privacy policies. The Site operator does not control or access this data.

Original commentary, the Anomalousness Index scoring rubric, audit pages, and software code on the Site are the work of the operator and are made available under terms documented in the public repository.

8. Data subject rights (GDPR / CCPA)

Because the Site collects no personally identifiable information through Plausible and currently runs no advertising or email systems, there is generally no personal data to access, correct, or delete. If you believe the Site holds personal data about you (for example, in correspondence you have sent to the operator), email anthony.fong.esq@gmail.com with a description of what you are requesting. Verified requests will be processed within 30 days.

9. Children's privacy

The Site is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from them. If you believe a child has provided information through this Site, contact the operator and it will be removed.

10. DMCA / takedown / corrections

The Site mirrors public-domain government files and does not host third-party copyrighted content. If you believe content on the Site infringes a right you hold, or if you have identified a factual error on any analytical page (scoring, audit log, methodology), email anthony.fong.esq@gmail.com with specifics. Documented corrections to data claims are logged transparently on the relevant page (see /revisions for an example).

Material changes (such as activating AdSense or launching an email list) will be reflected here with a new "Last updated" date at the top of the page. Significant changes will also be announced on the homepage. Continued use of the Site after a policy update constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
