TL;DR (plain English)

War.gov changed how they label their UFO file list on May 11, 2026, but the files themselves are exactly the same. 130 PDFs and 28 videos exist in both versions. The arithmetic

The row-count delta of -3 (161 May 8 rows -> 158 current rows) decomposes cleanly:

May 8 CSV: 161 rows = 130 unique PDFs + 16 video rows + 15 PDF rows that were duplicates within the May 8 file = 130 PDF URLs + 28 video DVIDS IDs in the unique set May 11+ CSV: 158 rows = 130 unique PDFs + 16 video rows + 12 additional cross-reference PDF rows = 130 PDF URLs + 28 video DVIDS IDs in the unique set (same unique set after accounting for 1 PDF slug change; see Table 2 below) Net delta: -3 rows = 15 historical PDF-row duplicates - 12 new cross-reference rows = -3

The unique-file inventory between the two dates is identical. The CSV structure changed; the file set did not.

How to verify this page yourself

Anyone can re-run the diff. The inputs are public:

  1. Current war.gov CSV: war.gov/Portals/1/Interactive/2026/UFO/uap-csv.csv (warm a session at war.gov/UFO/ first or the CDN will 403)
  2. May 8 manifest snapshot: /generated/api/files.json (our parsed copy; the original war.gov May 8 CSV is no longer hosted but its SHA-256 is preserved in our poll-state history)
  3. The diff itself: extract the PDF | Image Link column from the CSV, normalize to lowercase, set-difference against the source_url field from our manifest. The result will match the tables on this page.

Why this page exists

An earlier version of /revisions characterized the May 11 change as including removals, additions, geographic consolidation, and reclassification. None of those characterizations are supported by the URL-set comparison. Rather than silently rewriting and pretending the mistake didn't happen, we kept the correction visible on /revisions and built this page to host the verified data tables. Per the site's editorial policy, transparency about our own errors is part of the credibility moat.
