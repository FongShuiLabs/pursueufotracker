State Department UAP Diplomatic Cables

7 files Source: war.gov/UFO Released: May 8, 2026 License: 17 U.S.C. § 105 (public domain)

Seven U.S. Department of State diplomatic cables were declassified under PURSUE, spanning 1952 through 2025. They include the 1985 Papua New Guinea cable from the U.S. Embassy in Port Moresby to USCINCPAC, the 1994 Tajikistan PanAm crew sighting at flight level 410, plus dispatches from Kazakhstan, Turkmenistan, Georgia, and Mexico. These cables are the State Department's running record of what U.S. diplomats reported back to Foggy Bottom when foreign governments or aviation authorities raised UAP encounters through diplomatic channels.
