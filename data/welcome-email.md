## Operator's notes

- Subject line tested for high open rate ("you're in" framing + curiosity gap)
- Reply-CTA at bottom is intentional — replies improve sender reputation and primary-tab placement
- Forward-to-friend ask is the highest-leverage growth lever per email
- Closing P.S. invites correction — feedback loop for site quality + builds parasocial trust

## When to revise

- After 3 drops, refresh the welcome email with current stats (drop count, file count)
- If you ever turn on ads/sponsorship, REWRITE the "What this site is NOT" section honestly
- If a journalist cites the site in a major outlet, add their citation to the welcome email as social proof
